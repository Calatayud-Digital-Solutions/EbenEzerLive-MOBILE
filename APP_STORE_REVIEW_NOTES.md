# Notes for App Store Review (Guideline 4.2.2)

Use the text below in **App Store Connect → Your App → App Review Information → Notes** when resubmitting.

---

**App purpose and main functionality**

This app provides **live real-time audio translation** during religious services. It is the primary way our community receives the service in their language.

- **Context:** Our church holds services in Romanian for about 1,000 people. Many attendees and online viewers speak Spanish or English and need live translation.
- **Core functionality:** Professional translators broadcast their voice in real time via WebRTC. Users open the app, choose their language (Spanish, English, or Romanian), and hear the translation with minimal delay. This is not pre-recorded content or a simple link to a website.
- **Use cases:**
  1. **In person:** Attendees use the app on their phone with headphones to listen to the translator while the main service is in Romanian.
  2. **Online:** We show a QR code on our YouTube stream so anyone worldwide can open the app, scan the QR, and listen to the translation while watching the video.

The app is therefore a **live translation tool** for a specific community, not a marketing or informational site. The main screen is focused on choosing the language and listening to the stream; schedule and contact details are in a secondary “Schedule & contact” screen for those who need them.

We have restructured the app so that:
- The home screen clearly presents “Live translation” and explains that users can listen in their language at church or while watching YouTube.
- Schedule and contact information are in a separate “Schedule & contact” section so the primary experience is the translation feature.

**How to test the language buttons (Guideline 2.1):**  
The language buttons are only **active during the live stream**. If you open the app when no service is being streamed, you will see all three languages with a red indicator and a clear message: "No languages available yet — Translation is only available during the live stream…" with instructions to use "Schedule & contact" → "Request technical support" for questions. To test actually selecting a language, please use the app **while our YouTube stream is live** (e.g. Sundays 10:00 or 18:00, Tue/Thu 20:00 CET) or contact us via the in-app "Request technical support" and we can arrange a short test stream for review.

Thank you for reviewing our app.
